About

For centuries, whispers of enchanted potions have quietly circulated through hidden covens and dusty tomes. Founded in the early 1600s—during the height of witch hysteria in North America—The Obsidian Phial was forced to cloak its brews in secrecy for fear of persecution.

Now, over 300 years later, we are able to be out in the open and supply our wonderful potions that can aid with a variety of things, this includes low energy, bad luck, and sleeplessness. Take a look at our shop and see how we can help you!
